CAR INFO

ROLLS ROYCE GHOST

ENGINE

The Rolls-Royce Ghost is powered by a 6.75-liter twin-turbo V12 engine that delivers 563 horsepower. It provides effortless acceleration with whisper-quiet operation, ensuring a smooth and commanding ride.

WHEELS & TIRES

Refined alloy wheels paired with custom-developed, noise-reducing tires absorb road imperfections and maintain a silent, smooth glide over any surface.

STEERING

Electric power-assisted steering with rear-wheel steering enhances low-speed maneuverability and provides exceptional stability at higher speeds.

SEATS

Hand-stitched leather seats with heating, ventilation, and massage functions provide maximum comfort, transforming every journey into a first-class experience.

BRAKES

Smooth, powerful braking ensures confident stopping power while preserving the serene cabin environment.

DRIVING TECHNOLOGY

Features like Planar Suspension System, Satellite Aided Transmission, and adaptive cruise control use GPS and real-time data to prepare for the road ahead, delivering unmatched ride intelligence and refinement.
