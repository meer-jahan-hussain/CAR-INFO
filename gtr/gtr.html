CAR INFO

NISSAN GT-R R35

ENGINE

The Nissan GT-R is powered by a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine, famously known as the VR38DETT. This hand-built engine produces around 565 horsepower, while the NISMO version delivers up to 600 horsepower. The GT-R can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in about 2.9 seconds, making it one of the fastest production cars in the world. The engine is paired with a 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, delivering lightning-fast gear shifts and exceptional performance.

WHEELS & TIRES

The Nissan GT-R comes equipped with 20-inch forged alloy wheels, designed for strength and lightweight performance. It uses high-performance tires (such as Dunlop or Michelin, depending on variant) that provide excellent grip and stability at high speeds. The wide tire setup ensures strong traction during acceleration, cornering, and braking, giving the GT-R its legendary road-holding ability.

STEERING

The GT-R features an advanced electronic power-assisted rack-and-pinion steering system. The steering is precise and responsive, offering excellent feedback to the driver. Multiple driving modes allow the steering feel to be adjusted between Comfort, Normal, and R-Mode, making the car suitable for daily driving as well as aggressive track use.

SEATS

Inside, the Nissan GT-R offers sport bucket seats designed to provide strong support during high-speed driving and cornering. The seats are wrapped in premium leather and Alcantara, combining comfort with a sporty feel. The driver-focused cockpit layout ensures easy access to controls, while the seating position enhances confidence and control behind the wheel.

BRAKES

The GT-R is equipped with a high-performance Brembo braking system, featuring large ventilated disc brakes on all four wheels. These brakes provide powerful and consistent stopping performance, even under extreme driving conditions. Advanced systems such as ABS and electronic brakeforce distribution help maintain control and stability during hard braking.

DRIVING TECHNOLOGY

Nissan GT-R is packed with advanced driving technology, including its famous ATTESA E-TS all-wheel-drive system, which automatically distributes power between the front and rear wheels for maximum traction. The car also features adjustable suspension settings, launch control, traction control, and stability management systems. A multifunction performance display provides real-time data such as boost pressure, G-forces, and lap times, enhancing the driving experience for both road and track use.
