CAR INFO

ROLLS ROYCE PHANTOM

ENGINE

The Rolls-Royce Phantom is equipped with a 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine that produces around 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. It delivers smooth, near-silent acceleration with effortless power delivery, staying true to the brand’s signature "waftability".

WHEELS & TIRES

Large, polished alloy wheels—typically 21 or 22 inches—paired with specially designed, noise-reducing tires that include a layer of foam inside to minimize road noise, enhancing the serene cabin experience.

STEERING

Electrically assisted power steering provides light, effortless turning at low speeds and firm, confident handling at high speeds. Rear-wheel steering improves agility in tight spaces and increases highway stability.

SEATS

Fully customizable, luxurious seats with heating, cooling, massage functions, and memory settings ensure maximum comfort for all passengers, especially in rear executive-class seating.

BRAKES

Large ventilated disc brakes with ABS, electronic brake-force distribution, and regenerative braking ensure smooth, safe, and quiet deceleration.

DRIVING TECHNOLOGY

Cutting-edge systems like adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, head-up display, night vision, and the "Flagbearer" system scan the road ahead and adjust the suspension preemptively, delivering the famously smooth and intelligent ride Rolls-Royce is known for.
