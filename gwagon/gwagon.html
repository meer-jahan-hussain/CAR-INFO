CAR INFO

MERCEDES AMG G 63

ENGINE

The Mercedes-AMG G 63 is powered by a 4.0-liter V8 Biturbo engine, delivering 577 horsepower and 850 Nm of torque. This powerful engine allows the SUV to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 4.5 seconds. It comes with a 9-speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT TCT automatic transmission that ensures smooth and aggressive gear shifts. With the AMG Driverâ€™s Package, the top speed reaches 240 km/h, which is impressive for such a heavy luxury SUV.

WHEELS & TIRES

The G 63 comes standard with 20-inch AMG alloy wheels, while optional 21- or 22-inch wheels are available. The tires are 275/50 R20 high-performance tires, designed to provide strong grip on both road and off-road surfaces. The wide tires not only improve stability but also give the SUV an aggressive and commanding stance, perfect for high-speed driving or rough terrains.

STEERING

This SUV features AMG Performance electric power steering, optimized for both comfort and sporty driving. The steering offers multiple modes: Comfort, Sport, and Sport+, allowing the driver to adjust responsiveness based on driving style. It delivers precise handling on highways while providing strong control during off-road adventures.

SEATS

Inside, the G 63 features premium leather seats that combine luxury with comfort. The front seats are electrically adjustable and come with heating and ventilation, ensuring comfort in any climate. The rear seats offer ample legroom for adult passengers, and AMG-specific design touches like embossed logos and contrast stitching add a sporty, upscale look.

BRAKES

The G 63 is equipped with a high-performance AMG braking system to handle the heavy SUV efficiently. Both front and rear brakes are ventilated discs made from high-performance materials, ensuring stability and safety during sudden stops or high-speed braking. The system also includes ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) and can be fine-tuned using AMG Dynamic Select for optimal response in any driving condition.

DRIVING TECHNOLOGY

The Mercedes-AMG G 63 is packed with advanced driving assistance and technology features. It includes AMG Dynamic Select, which allows customization of driving modes such as Comfort, Sport, Sport+, Individual, and Off-road. Safety features include adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, blind-spot monitoring, and traffic sign recognition. Off-road capabilities are enhanced by three differential locks and a low-range gearbox, allowing the SUV to tackle rough terrain with ease. For infotainment, the MBUX system provides a high-resolution touchscreen and voice control, making the driving experience modern, convenient, and connected.
