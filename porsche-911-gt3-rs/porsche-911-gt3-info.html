CAR INFO

PORSCHE 911 GT3 RS

ENGINE

The 911 GT3 RS is powered by a 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-six engine delivering over 500 horsepower and revving up to 9,000 rpm. Instant throttle response and motorsport-inspired sound define its performance character.

WHEELS & TIRES

Lightweight, center-lock forged alloy wheels with Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 tires provide exceptional grip, stability, and confidence during high-speed cornering and track driving.

STEERING

Rear-axle steering enhances maneuverability at low speeds and stability at high speeds, providing direct feedback for precise control on every turn.

SEATS

Full carbon fiber bucket seats offer lateral support and reduce weight, keeping the driver secure during intense driving sessions.

BRAKES

High-performance Porsche Ceramic Composite Brakes (PCCB) deliver exceptional stopping power, reduced fade, and lighter weight, ideal for extended track use.

DRIVING TECHNOLOGY

Advanced systems like Porsche Active Suspension Management (PASM), Porsche Stability Management (PSM), and adjustable drive modes optimize handling, traction, and responsiveness for road and track performance.
