CAR INFO

PORSCHE 911 GT3 RS

ENGINE

The 911 GT3 RS is powered by a 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-six engine that delivers over 500 horsepower and revs to an exhilarating 9,000 rpm. It offers instant throttle response and a motorsport-inspired sound that defines its performance character.

WHEELS & TIRES

The car rides on lightweight, center-lock forged alloy wheels fitted with Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 tires. This combination provides incredible grip, stability, and confidence, especially during high-speed cornering and track driving.

STEERING

Precision steering is a highlight of the GT3 RS, featuring rear-axle steering for enhanced maneuverability at low speeds and improved stability at high speeds. The system offers direct feedback, allowing for razor-sharp control on every turn.

SEATS

Inside, the GT3 RS comes equipped with full carbon fiber bucket seats that offer exceptional lateral support and help reduce weight. Designed for track use, they keep the driver firmly in place during intense driving sessions.

The GT3 RS features advanced systems like Porsche Active Suspension Management (PASM), Porsche Stability Management (PSM), and adjustable drive modes. These technologies work together to optimize handling, traction, and responsiveness for both road and track performance.
