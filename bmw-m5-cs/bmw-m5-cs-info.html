BMW M5 CS

ENGINE

The BMW M5 CS is powered by a 4.4-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo V8 engine, delivering an astonishing 627 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. This powerhouse propels the M5 CS from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.9 seconds, making it the most powerful and quickest BMW production car to date. Its precision-tuned performance and lightweight components offer an unmatched blend of raw power and refined engineering.

WHEELS & TIRES

The M5 CS rides on exclusive 20-inch forged alloy wheels, finished in a striking Gold Bronze hue. These wheels are wrapped in high-performance Pirelli P Zero Corsa tires, specifically developed for optimal track and road performance. Designed to maximize grip, stability, and responsiveness, this setup enhances cornering confidence and high-speed control.
