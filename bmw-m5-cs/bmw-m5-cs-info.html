CAR INFO

BMW M5 CS

ENGINE

The BMW M5 CS is powered by a 4.4-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo V8 engine, delivering an astonishing 627 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. This powerhouse propels the M5 CS from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.9 seconds, making it the most powerful and quickest BMW production car to date. Its precision-tuned performance and lightweight components offer an unmatched blend of raw power and refined engineering.

WHEELS & TIRES

The M5 CS rides on exclusive 20-inch forged alloy wheels, finished in a striking Gold Bronze hue. These wheels are wrapped in high-performance Pirelli P Zero Corsa tires, specifically developed for optimal track and road performance. Designed to maximize grip, stability, and responsiveness, this setup enhances cornering confidence and high-speed control.

STEERING

BMW’s M Servotronic steering system in the M5 CS provides a finely tuned blend of precision and feedback. With variable steering ratio, the system adjusts to driving conditions for agile response at low speeds and rock-solid stability at high speeds. Whether carving through backroads or cruising the autobahn, the steering delivers a direct connection between driver and road.

SEATS

Inside, the M5 CS features exclusive M Carbon bucket seats for the driver and front passenger, offering lightweight construction, aggressive bolstering, and motorsport-inspired aesthetics. Rear passengers enjoy individual sport seats for a true four-seater layout. Wrapped in Merino leather with contrasting red accents and CS badging, the seats elevate the cabin’s sporty and luxurious feel.

BRAKES

Braking performance in the M5 CS is handled by standard M Carbon Ceramic brakes, identifiable by their gold-painted calipers. These brakes provide exceptional stopping power, fade resistance, and weight savings compared to traditional systems, ensuring the M5 CS can decelerate as confidently as it accelerates—even under extreme track conditions.
