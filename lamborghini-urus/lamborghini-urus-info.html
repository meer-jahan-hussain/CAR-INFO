CAR INFO

LAMBORGHINI URUS

ENGINE

The Lamborghini Urus is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces an exhilarating 657 horsepower and 850 Nm of torque. This powerhouse propels the Urus from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.3 seconds, making it one of the fastest SUVs in the world. Engineered for high performance and smooth power delivery, the V8 delivers relentless acceleration, a deep exhaust note, and the thrill of a true Lamborghini supercar in an SUV body.

WHEELS & TIRES

The Urus rides on bold 21-inch to optional 23-inch alloy wheels that not only enhance its muscular stance but also improve aerodynamics and handling. Wrapped in specially developed Pirelli P Zero tires, the setup delivers superior grip, precise cornering, and excellent road feedback. Whether you’re carving through mountain roads or cruising on the highway, the wheels and tires are engineered for performance and durability in every driving condition.

STEERING

The Lamborghini Urus features an advanced electromechanical power steering system paired with rear-wheel steering for outstanding agility and stability. At low speeds, the rear wheels turn in the opposite direction of the front for tighter turning, while at high speeds, they turn in the same direction for smoother lane changes and cornering. The steering is sharp, responsive, and finely tuned to give the driver a confident, connected feel at all times.

SEATS

Inside the Urus, the seating strikes a perfect balance between race-inspired design and first-class comfort. The standard front sport seats offer 12-way power adjustment, with optional heated, ventilated, and massage functions for added luxury. Finished in premium leather with Lamborghini’s signature stitching and customization options, the seats provide both the ergonomic support for spirited driving and the comfort needed for long journeys.

BRAKES

Braking performance in the Lamborghini Urus is unmatched, thanks to the inclusion of massive carbon-ceramic disc brakes—the largest ever fitted to an SUV. These high-performance brakes offer exceptional stopping power, fade resistance, and thermal stability, even under extreme driving conditions. Whether navigating traffic or charging down a mountain road, the Urus’s braking system delivers precise control and confidence-inspiring performance.

DRIVING TECNOLOGY

The Urus is packed with cutting-edge driving technology designed to adapt to every environment. The ANIMA drive mode selector allows you to toggle between Strada, Sport, Corsa, Sabbia (sand), Terra (off-road), and Neve (snow) modes, optimizing the vehicle’s performance based on terrain and driving style. Features like adaptive air suspension, active torque vectoring, and electronic stability systems work seamlessly together to provide a smooth, agile, and commanding driving experience—on or off the road.
