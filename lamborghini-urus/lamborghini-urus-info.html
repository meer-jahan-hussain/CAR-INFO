CAR INFO

LAMBORGHINI URUS

ENGINE

The Lamborghini Urus is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 657 horsepower and 850 Nm of torque. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.3 seconds, making it one of the fastest SUVs in the world. Engineered for high performance, smooth power delivery, and a thrilling exhaust note, the Urus delivers supercar-level excitement in an SUV body.

WHEELS & TIRES

The Urus rides on bold 21-inch to optional 23-inch alloy wheels, enhancing its muscular stance and aerodynamics. Wrapped in specially developed Pirelli P Zero tires, the setup provides superior grip, precise cornering, and excellent road feedback for both mountain roads and highways.

STEERING

The Urus features advanced electromechanical power steering with rear-wheel steering. At low speeds, rear wheels turn opposite to the front for tight turns; at high speeds, they turn the same direction for stability. The steering is sharp, responsive, and finely tuned, giving the driver a confident, connected feel.

SEATS

The Urus seats balance race-inspired design with first-class comfort. Front sport seats feature 12-way power adjustment, with optional heated, ventilated, and massage functions. Finished in premium leather with Lamborghini stitching, the seats provide ergonomic support for spirited driving and comfort for long journeys.

DRIVING TECHNOLOGY

The Urus includes cutting-edge driving technology. ANIMA drive mode selector offers Strada, Sport, Corsa, Sabbia (sand), Terra (off-road), and Neve (snow) modes. Adaptive air suspension, active torque vectoring, and electronic stability systems provide a smooth, agile, and commanding driving experience on or off-road.
