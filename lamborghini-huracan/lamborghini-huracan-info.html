CAR INFO

LAMBORGHINI HURACAN

ENGINE

The Lamborghini Huracán is powered by a breathtaking 5.2-liter naturally aspirated V10 engine, delivering up to 640 horsepower and lightning-fast throttle response. This engine launches the Huracán from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.9 seconds. Engineered for both track dominance and road-driving thrills, its high-revving character and unmistakable exhaust note define the pure, emotional driving experience Lamborghini is known for. Available in Huracán EVO, STO, and Tecnica variants, this V10 engine is a masterpiece of automotive performance.

WHEELS & TIRES

The Huracán features high-performance wheels and tires designed for exceptional grip, handling, and style. Lightweight alloy wheels enhance aerodynamics and brake cooling, paired with specially developed Pirelli P Zero tires optimized for maximum traction and responsiveness. This precision-matched setup ensures razor-sharp cornering and confidence-inspiring stability, embodying Lamborghini’s relentless pursuit of performance and design excellence.

STEERING

The Huracán is equipped with advanced steering for precise, responsive control. Lamborghini Dynamic Steering (LDS) adapts in real time to driving conditions and speed, providing sharper handling at low speeds and enhanced stability at high speeds. Combined with a lightweight chassis and performance-tuned suspension, the steering ensures exceptional feedback and agility, keeping the driver fully connected to the road.

SEATS

The Huracán’s seats combine luxurious comfort with race-inspired support. Designed to keep the driver firmly in place during high-performance driving, they come in premium materials such as leather and Alcantara, with customizable stitching and colors. Ergonomically contoured for long-distance comfort, the seats balance elegance with functionality, reflecting Lamborghini’s commitment to performance-driven luxury.

BRAKES

The Huracán is equipped with a state-of-the-art braking system, featuring carbon-ceramic disc brakes for unmatched stopping power and control. Large ventilated rotors and advanced calipers ensure fade-resistant performance on both road and track. Engineered for high-performance driving, the brakes provide precise modulation and rapid response, giving drivers confidence to push the car to its limits.

DRIVING TECHNOLOGY

The Huracán is packed with cutting-edge driving technology to enhance performance, safety, and driver engagement. At the heart is Lamborghini’s ANIMA (Adaptive Network Intelligent Management), allowing drivers to select from Strada, Sport, and Corsa modes. Advanced traction control, torque vectoring, and optional Lamborghini Integrated Vehicle Dynamics (LDVI) continuously adjust the car’s setup for optimal performance. Every system ensures a thrilling, intuitive, and highly connected driving experience.
