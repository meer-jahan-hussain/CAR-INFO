CAR INFO

KOENIGSEGG JESKO

ENGINE

The Koenigsegg Jesko is powered by a 5.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, engineered entirely in-house by Koenigsegg. It produces 1,280 horsepower on regular fuel, and up to 1,600 horsepower on E85 biofuel. The engine revs up to 8,500 rpm, delivering razor-sharp throttle response and extreme acceleration, making Jesko one of the most powerful production cars ever.

WHEELS & TIRES

Jesko uses lightweight forged aluminum or optional carbon-fiber wheels to reduce unsprung weight and improve handling. Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 or Cup 2 R tires provide maximum grip at extreme speeds, ensuring excellent traction, stability, and cornering performance even under brutal acceleration or braking.

STEERING

Jesko features precision rack-and-pinion steering with electronic power assistance. It is extremely responsive and direct, giving precise feedback at both low and ultra-high speeds. The system is tuned for track-level accuracy, allowing confident handling during aggressive cornering and high-speed driving.

SEATS

Jesko’s seats are lightweight carbon-fiber racing seats, wrapped in premium leather and Alcantara. They keep the driver secure during extreme acceleration and cornering, while providing a refined, driver-focused cabin with high-quality materials and modern digital displays.

BRAKES

Jesko uses an advanced carbon-ceramic braking system with large ventilated discs and high-performance calipers. This setup ensures fade-free braking even during repeated high-speed stops, providing safety and confidence for both road and track conditions.

DRIVING TECHNOLOGY

Jesko showcases cutting-edge technology, including the Light Speed Transmission (LST) — a 9-speed multi-clutch gearbox for near-instant gear changes. It features active aerodynamics, adaptive suspension, multiple driving modes, traction and stability control systems, a digital instrument cluster, infotainment system, and smart driver-assistance technologies.
