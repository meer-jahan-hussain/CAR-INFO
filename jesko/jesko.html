CAR INFO

KOENIGSEGG JESKO

ENGINE

The Koenigsegg Jesko is powered by a 5.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, engineered entirely in-house by Koenigsegg. This extreme hypercar produces 1,280 horsepower on regular fuel, and up to 1,600 horsepower when running on E85 biofuel. The engine is designed for very high revs, reaching 8,500 rpm, and delivers brutal acceleration with razor-sharp throttle response. Jesko is built for extreme performance, making it one of the most powerful production cars ever created.

WHEELS & TIRES

The Jesko uses lightweight forged aluminum or optional carbon-fiber wheels, designed to reduce unsprung weight and improve handling. It is fitted with Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 or Cup 2 R tires, providing maximum grip at very high speeds. The wide, track-focused tire setup ensures excellent traction, stability, and cornering performance, even under extreme acceleration and braking conditions.

STEERING

Koenigsegg Jesko features a precision rack-and-pinion steering system with electronic power assistance. The steering is extremely responsive and direct, giving the driver precise feedback and control at both low and ultra-high speeds. The system is tuned for track-level accuracy, allowing confident handling during aggressive cornering and high-speed driving.

SEATS

Inside the Jesko, the seats are lightweight carbon-fiber racing seats, designed to keep the driver firmly in place during extreme acceleration and cornering. They are wrapped in premium leather and Alcantara, combining motorsport functionality with luxury craftsmanship. Despite being a hardcore hypercar, the cabin offers a refined and driver-focused environment with high-quality materials and modern digital displays.

BRAKES

The Jesko is equipped with an advanced carbon-ceramic braking system, delivering exceptional stopping power. Large ventilated discs with high-performance brake calipers ensure fade-free braking even during repeated high-speed stops. This braking setup is essential for controlling a car capable of extreme speeds and provides confidence and safety on both road and track.
