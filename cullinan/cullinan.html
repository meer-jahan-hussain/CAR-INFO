CAR INFO

ROLLS ROYCE CULLINAN

ENGINE

The Rolls‑Royce Cullinan is powered by a 6.7‑liter twin‑turbo V12 petrol engine producing 563 horsepower and 850 Nm of torque. This massive engine allows the SUV to accelerate from 0‑100 km/h in 5.2‑5.3 seconds. It is paired with an 8‑speed automatic transmission, delivering smooth and refined power for effortless cruising.

WHEELS & TIRES

Cullinan rolls on 22‑inch alloy wheels fitted with high‑performance tires (P255/45R22 front, P285/40R22 rear). Optional custom wheels are available. These wheels provide a confident stance, excellent grip on paved roads and light off-road surfaces, and complement the SUV's elegant proportions.

STEERING

The Cullinan features rack‑and‑pinion steering with electro‑hydraulic assist and four‑wheel steering for better maneuverability and stability. This setup ensures smooth and precise control whether in city traffic or cruising on highways.

SEATS

The Cullinan’s interior features handcrafted premium leather seats with heating, ventilation, and optional massage function. Spacious front and rear cabins provide ultimate comfort, while bespoke trims and optional starlight headliner enhance luxury.

BRAKES

High‑performance ventilated disc brakes on all wheels with ABS and stability controls provide smooth yet powerful stopping force. Advanced traction and braking electronics ensure confidence-inspiring safety even for a nearly three-ton SUV.

DRIVING TECHNOLOGY

Cullinan offers advanced technology including adaptive self‑levelling air suspension, multiple driving modes, off-road mode, adaptive cruise control, blind-spot monitoring, lane-departure warning, surround cameras, head-up display, and premium infotainment. The Flagbearer system uses forward-looking cameras to adjust suspension and ride dynamics for unmatched comfort.
