ROLLS ROYCE CULLINAN

ENGINE

The Rolls‑Royce Cullinan is powered by a 6.7‑liter twin‑turbo V12 petrol engine that produces around 563 horsepower and approximately 850 Nm of torque. This massive V12 delivers effortless and refined power, enabling this luxury SUV to accelerate from 0‑100 km/h in about 5.2‑5.3 seconds despite its substantial weight. The engine is paired with an 8‑speed automatic transmission with smooth shift logic, perfectly matching Cullinan’s ultra‑premium character. The performance is strong yet calm — providing both powerful acceleration and serene cruising at highway speeds.

WHEELS & TIRES

Cullinan typically rolls on 22‑inch alloy wheels fitted with high‑performance tires (e.g., P255/45R22 front and P285/40R22 rear), giving it a confident stance and ensuring excellent grip on both paved roads and light off‑road surfaces. The large wheel size enhances its elegant proportions while providing stability and comfort, and optional custom wheel sizes are available from Rolls‑Royce Bespoke.

STEERING

The SUV features a rack‑and‑pinion steering system with electro‑hydraulic assist, giving precise and smooth control. Rolls‑Royce also integrates four‑wheel steering to improve maneuverability in tight spaces and stability at higher speeds — impressive for such a large vehicle. This advanced steering setup ensures confidence‑inspiring handling whether driving in a city or cruising on a highway.

SEATS

True to Rolls‑Royce luxury, the Cullinan’s interior boasts handcrafted premium leather seats with generous cushioning, heating, and optional ventilation. The front and rear cabin space is exceptionally roomy, and the seats are designed for ultimate comfort on long journeys. Optional features like rear massage seats and bespoke interior trims (including starlight headliner, custom wood, or metal accents) elevate the cabin to one of the most luxurious in any SUV. BRAKES

Cullinan comes equipped with high‑performance ventilated disc brakes on all four wheels, supported by ABS (Anti‑Lock Braking System) and advanced stability controls. These brakes provide smooth yet powerful stopping force — essential for a vehicle weighing nearly three tons. Combined with advanced traction and braking electronics, Cullinan delivers confidence‑inspiring safety and comfort in all driving conditions.

DRIVING TECHNOLOGY

Rolls‑Royce makes the Cullinan as technologically advanced as it is luxurious. It features adaptive self‑levelling air suspension with electronically controlled dampers that deliver the trademark Rolls‑Royce “magic carpet” ride — smoothing out bumps and road imperfections effortlessly. The SUV also has multiple driving modes, including an off‑road mode that raises suspension height for rough terrain. Technology like adaptive cruise control, blind‑spot monitoring, lane‑departure warning, surround‑view cameras, head‑up display, and advanced infotainment with connectivity features create a safe and enjoyable driving environment. The “Flagbearer” system uses forward‑looking cameras to adjust suspension and ride dynamics proactively, further enhancing comfort.
